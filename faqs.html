FAQs

Who are we?

UniteSync connects volunteers to Non-Profit Organizations to intensify the volunteering culture in India. We also bring together different organizations working for different causes across the nation and help them collaborate with each other.

Do I have to sign up?

You are free to look at volunteering opportunities on our homepage, however, both Volunteers and Non-Profit Organizations have to sign up to post for jobs, events, free positions, and also to apply.

Do I have to pay to use UniteSync?

No, UniteSync is a Non-Profit Organization which does not charge anything for its services.

Who should I contact in case of support.

Just hit us up at unitesyncllc@gmail.com
